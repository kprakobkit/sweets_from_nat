It's not common that people go out and splurge on healthy sweets. Let's admit it, "healthy" sounds unappealing when you are describing them. I love to bake, but am always concerned about all the sugar, butter, and flour that would go into it. I don't want to just use less sugar and butter so that it would be less unhealthy. I want to actually make it healthy!

Fortunately, I found ways to use ingredients that are actually good for you, while not compromising on the taste. That is why you will find goodies like nuts, flax meal, and coconut oil in our sweets. It's so good you'll forget that it's healthy.

- Natalie Pham, Founder
